A short documentary about two cinemas which were located in Prizren, Serbia
(Kosovo). From the time of Yugoslavia, to the present day, and the impact
they've had on the town, the people, and the collective unconscious.

The documentary posseses a unique style, blending both objective perspective
with archive material, and the interviews of people whose lives were shaped
by the cinemas, along with subjective narration from the eyes of a lyrical
subject who was present and together with the cinemas through each point in
their history.
